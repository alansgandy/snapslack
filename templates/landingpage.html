Snapslack

This is a simple Snapchat™ replica for Slack™

Using the "/snapslack" command, send you message to the current channel or message group and watch it dissapear after 10 seconds.
A countdown timer shows you how long the message will remain visible.



Snapslack also accepts the "/giphy" command. Ex: "/snapslack /giphy taco" will send a taco gif to the current channel or message group. (use /giphyr for r rated content)
